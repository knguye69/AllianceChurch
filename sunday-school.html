Sunday School

At our Community, we have “Morning Sunday School” from 10:30am - 12:00am. We divide into 5 different groups according to the age. We have:

  • Children group from 6 to 10 year of age.
  • Teenager group from 11 to 15 year of age.
  • Youth group from 16-30 year of age.
  • Adult group from 31-49 year of age.
  • Special group from 50 and up.

Within an hour, we learn about Bible and share with each other about faith and life. T hese group is very meaningful and helpful to grow up and become mature in Chirst.

Our mission

Our mission is to spread Good News to everybody around the world through God's love.

We also devote our time into Mission Trips every year to reach out to people in different countries who are in needs of physically, emotionally, and spiritually.​

We acknowledge the importance of worshiping​ and sharing our time altogether.

We would love to go around the world to share about Jesus Christ and help as many non-believers out there as possible​ to come back to our God.
