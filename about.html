About Us

Our community started in early 2000. There was not many Christians back then. However, we prayed and continued our hard works and now we have about 100 Christians attending at our community. We have important roles such as: Pastor, secretary, treasurer, and bible teachers. Our recent Pastor is Nhut Nguyen.

Our mission

Our mission is to spread Good News to everybody around the world through God's love.

We also devote our time into Mission Trips every year to reach out to people in different countries who are in needs of physically, emotionally, and spiritually.​

We acknowledge the importance of worshiping​ and sharing our time altogether.

We would love to go around the world to share about Jesus Christ and help as many non-believers out there as possible​ to come back to our God.
